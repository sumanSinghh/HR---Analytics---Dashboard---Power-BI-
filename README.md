## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and job roles. The dashboard helps HR teams identify trends and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition.
- Identify departments with the highest attrition.
- Understand employee demographics.
- Analyze salary distribution.
- Monitor workforce KPIs.

---

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs
- Total Employees
- Total Attrition
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visuals
- Attrition by Education
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Gender
- Department Filter (HR, R&D, Sales)

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- Highest attrition is observed among Laboratory Technicians.
- Employees earning up to 5K salary have the highest attrition.
- The 26–35 age group contributes the most to attrition.
- Research & Development department shows significant employee turnover.
- Life Sciences employees have the highest attrition among education fields.
